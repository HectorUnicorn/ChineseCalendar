Discrepancies Between Modern Calculation and Historical Data (1645 – 1911)

First draft: February 2019, Last Update: 23 January, 2022


This page was first created in February 2019 and the information was of little value after I studied the algorithms used to calculate the moon phases and solar terms in the Shixian astronomical system. Thus, the content was removed on January 23, 2022.

In December 20220, I created a web-based platform to calculate the solar and lunar positions as well as the times of solar terms and moon phases according to the Shixian system. It may be useful for people interested in the study of the history of Chinese astronomy in the Qing dynasty.

A detailed comparison of the positions of the Sun and Moon, as well as the times of moon phases and solar terms, between the Shixian system and modern high-accuracy ephemerides can be found in Section 6 of this pdf document.
